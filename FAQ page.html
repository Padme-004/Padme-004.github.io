As we have ascertained from our survey, some of us have undergone the unpleasant sensation of anxiety, or its heightened state- a panic attack.
It is easy, with a bit of practise of regulated breathing to calm down. Use this gif when you're feeling anxious in the following manner.
Breathe in for the entire duration of the time that the figure is enlarging, pause and then give a long exhale till the figure collapses.
All is well.

Mental health refers to the emotional and psychological well-being and balance of a person.
Too often we go about life looking only at its gross aspects. The subtler workings of Life on our mind are ignored until one day, inevitably, they erupt out of us in word or deed.
We must take mental health into consideration to prevent this explosive reaction- which leads to violent emotions of sorrow, hopelessness, delusion or anxiety from overtaking us.
To be free, truly free, we must each one of us liberate our minds from the things that plague it, and mental health is the door that opens up the path to do that.

Depression differs from sadness.
It is normal to feel sad for a while, once in a while.
But when there is a persisting feeling of sadness, with or without a "valid" cause, then there is a chance that it may be depression. It is not a thing to be taken lightly- if you think you may be suffering a bout of it, or you know somebody who does, please- talk to a trusted friend or advisor. Be easy on your mind and seek to overcome it by passing through. Seek help- there is no shame in needing help- it is in fact the very basis from which life is nurtured.

Anxiety is a restlessness that leads to many physical symptoms. These physical symptoms include acute shortness of breath- which may lead to panting/gasping, rapid heartbeat, stress headaches, trembling hands and so on. Often, people suffering from anxiety mistake it to be some other physical ailment and fruitlessly undertake visit after visit to the doctor- only to be labelled hypochondriacs, or worse, receive the wrong medication. Meditation and mindfulness (awareness of the present moment) are the most trusted hacks for overcoming anxiety.
Therefore, just keep breathing.
