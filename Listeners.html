Welcome to the Listener's Section!

This section contains a list of resources that are free to access. Please keep in mind that these are not a substitute for professional help. That being said, if you, or someone you know is in need of a listening ear, or just some encouragement or advice- please check out these resources!

Who are Listeners and what is their role?

Listeners are volunteers, who will provide advice and support anonymously. Their email addresses, in no particular order are listed here. In case you wish to make use of this facility please keep the following things in mind:
1. Refrain from disclosing any senstive information, your personal details and any inappropriate messages.
2. The Listeners will get back to you in the span of a week (maximum).
